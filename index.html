    About Me :)

    I’m a Master’s student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UC San Diego, specializing in machine learning, computer vision, and multimodal AI. Currently, I work as a Research Assistant at the Statistical Visual Computing Lab, where I’m exploring amodal segmentation and object completion using vision-language models and deep generative frameworks. My previous research spans semantic segmentation, diffusion models, and transformer-based architectures—focused on building intelligent, scalable systems for image and language understanding. I’ve worked with PyTorch, HuggingFace, OpenCV, and containerized ML pipelines using Kubernetes and Slurm. I'm passionate about developing impactful AI systems that integrate vision, language, and reasoning—particularly those with real-world applications in scene understanding and generative AI. Let’s connect if you’re working on anything related to ML research, LLMs, vision-language models, or just want to talk about building cool things with AI!
